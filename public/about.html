About Us

Welcome to Pangasinan Treasure, your gateway to exploring and indulging in authentic local products from the beautiful province of Pangasinan, Philippines.

At Pangasinan Treasure, we are passionate about showcasing the rich cultural heritage and unique flavors that define our region. Our mission is to connect you with the finest handcrafted items and delectable delicacies that embody the spirit of Pangasinan.
